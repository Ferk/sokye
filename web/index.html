Level Information

How to Play

Goal

Push every box onto a goal tile. When all boxes are on goals, the level is complete.

Controls

Move with the arrow keys or WASD. Press U to undo your last move and R to reset the current level.

Tiles

Floor Open space you can walk across.
Wall Blocks both the player and boxes.
Goal Every box must finish on one of these tiles.
Player This is the character you control.
Box Push boxes around the map and onto goals.
Ice Players and boxes slide until they leave the ice or hit an obstacle.

Players and boxes can also appear on top of goal or ice tiles.
